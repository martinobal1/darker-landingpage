About me

I am a student of Applied Informatics, and as part of my studies and personal projects, I have developed a Google Chrome extension focused on providing a dark mode experience for web browsing. The primary function of this extension is to enhance readability and reduce eye strain by converting bright backgrounds into dark ones while ensuring that the text remains clearly visible by adjusting its color accordingly.
